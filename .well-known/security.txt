# Security Policy

This document outlines our security policy regarding vulnerabilities and exposure management.

1. **Reporting Security Vulnerabilities**  
   - If you believe you have discovered a vulnerability in our systems, please report it immediately to our security team via email at security@example.com.

2. **Response Times**  
   - We aim to acknowledge reports within 24 hours and will keep you updated throughout the process.

3. **Security Practices**  
   - Our company is committed to safeguarding user data and employs various measures to ensure the integrity and security of our systems.

4. **Public Disclosure**  
   - We welcome responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities and will credit those who report them in a timely manner.
